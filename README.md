## What I work on

I’m currently a Computer Science undergraduate at **The University of Texas at Arlington**
and a **Research Assistant with the USDA Agricultural Research Service (ARS)**, where I help
develop county-scale early-warning systems for **Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza (HPAI, H5N1)**.

My work focuses on:

- **Spatiotemporal forecasting under extreme imbalance** (~1% positives)
- **Leakage-safe evaluation** (walk-forward validation, strict temporal ordering)
- **Decision-oriented outputs** (calibrated risk tiers, and diagnostics over probabilities)
- **Interpretability & diagnostics** (calibration curves, error profiling, prevalence effects)
